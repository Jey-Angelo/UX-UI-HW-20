What I did:

UX Research, UI and interaction design

Challenge

We were challenged to modernize the outdated Loving Care Cat Rescue website, improving its usability and accessibility for all users.

Our Goals

  • Enhance visual appeal and user engagement by creating a custom UI style guide.
  • Improve accessibility for all users, and optimize the website for better search engine visibility.
  • highlight ways for users to contribute and support the organization, and streamline the adoption process.

Our Solution

Our solution involved a comprehensive redesign of the Loving Care Cat Rescue website, focusing on several key areas:

  • Cat Health Priority: We placed a strong emphasis on promoting the health and well-being of cats throughout the website, providing valuable information and resources to potential adopters.
  • User Help Emphasis: We redesigned the website to make it easier for users to find information about how they can help, whether through donations, volunteering, or fostering.
  • Adoption Process Improvement: We revamped the adoption process, making it more intuitive and user-friendly, with clear steps and requirements for potential adopters.
  • Accessibility Standards: We ensured that the website met modern accessibility standards, including proper use of alt text, keyboard navigation support, and color contrast considerations.
